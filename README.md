# David Power

AI Researcher | Healthcare Simulation | Machine Learning | Immersive Medical Education

I work at the intersection of **artificial intelligence, healthcare simulation, and immersive learning technologies**.

My research focuses on developing **AI-supported training systems** capable of modelling clinical communication, evaluating learner performance, and generating realistic training scenarios.

---

## Research Areas

### Generative AI for Clinical Simulation
- Multi-agent large language model systems for clinical communication
- Simulation of escalation-of-care conversations
- AI-supported tools for simulation faculty

### AI-Based Skill Assessment
- Automated assessment of procedural skills using deep learning
- Computer vision analysis of surgical performance
- Objective performance evaluation in simulation environments

### Virtual Reality and Immersive Medical Education
- Evaluation of VR learning environments for medical education
- Integration of immersive technologies in simulation-based training
- Research on adoption and effectiveness of VR teaching interventions

---

## Current Research Projects

**ESCALATE Dataset**  
Synthetic dataset of clinical escalation-of-care conversations generated using multi-agent large language models.

**Multi-Agent Clinical Simulation Framework**  
LLM-based agents modelling nurse–doctor communication during patient deterioration scenarios.

**AI Simulation Faculty Assistant**  
Generative AI system supporting simulation faculty during live training.

**Automated Surgical Skill Assessment**  
Deep learning models for analysing laparoscopic surgical performance.
